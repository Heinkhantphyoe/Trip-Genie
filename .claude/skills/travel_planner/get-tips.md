# Get Tips

Retrieve practical travel tips and advice for a destination.

## Usage

```
/get-tips <destination> <context>
```

## Parameters

- **destination** — the travel destination
- **context** — optional context to focus tips (e.g., "solo female traveler", "family with kids", "first time in Asia")

## Workflow

1. Gather destination-specific practical information
2. Organize tips by trip phase: before departure, arrival, during stay, departure
3. Tailor advice based on traveler context if provided
4. Prioritize tips that prevent common mistakes or frustration
5. Include specific recommendations (apps, phrases, locations)

## Output

Organized tips covering:
- **Documents & Entry** — visa, passport validity, arrival procedures
- **Money** — currency, ATMs, tipping customs, payment methods
- **Getting Around** — transit options, apps, safety
- **Culture & Etiquette** — dress codes, customs, dos and don'ts
- **Food & Water** — local specialties, dietary notes, water safety
- **Health & Safety** — vaccinations, emergency numbers, areas to avoid
- **Connectivity** — SIM cards, WiFi, useful apps
- **Packing** — climate-specific essentials, items to bring from home
