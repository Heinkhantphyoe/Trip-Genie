# Find Hotels

Search and compare accommodation options at a destination.

## Usage

```
/find-hotels <destination> <check-in> <check-out> <guests>
```

## Parameters

- **destination** — city, neighborhood, or landmark
- **check-in** — check-in date
- **check-out** — check-out date
- **guests** — number of guests and room configuration
- **budget** — max price per night (optional)
- **type** — hotel, hostel, apartment, resort, boutique (optional)

## Workflow

1. Search accommodations in the destination area
2. Filter by budget, type, and guest requirements
3. Evaluate options by location, reviews, amenities, and value
4. Identify proximity to key attractions and transit
5. Present curated shortlist across price tiers

## Output

A list of 5-8 accommodation options with:
- Name, type, and star rating
- Location and distance to city center
- Price per night and total stay cost
- Key amenities (WiFi, breakfast, pool, parking)
- Guest rating and review highlights
- Cancellation policy
