# TripGenie ✈️

## Your Smart Travel Planning Assistant

Plan your perfect trip with AI-powered recommendations for destinations, flights, hotels, and itineraries.

---

# 🎯 Problem

- Travel planning is **time-consuming** and **overwhelming**
- Jumping between multiple websites for flights, hotels, activities
- Hard to estimate realistic budgets
- Missing local tips and cultural insights

---

# 💡 Solution

**TripGenie** — an all-in-one AI travel planner that:

- 🔍 Searches destinations by climate, activities & budget
- ✈️ Compares flights and hotels in one place
- 📋 Generates day-by-day itineraries
- 💰 Estimates costs with savings tips
- 💡 Provides local travel tips & useful phrases

---

# 🛠️ Tech Stack

| Layer | Technology |
|-------|------------|
| Frontend | React 19 |
| Build | Vite 8 |
| Routing | React Router v7 |
| Styling | Tailwind CSS 4 |
| AI Agents | Research, Budget, Itinerary, Tips |

Modern, fast, and developer-friendly stack.

---

# 🔄 How It Works

1. **Choose Destination** — Pick where you want to go
2. **Set Travelers & Dates** — Customize your trip
3. **Get Travel Plan** — AI generates recommendations
4. **View Budget** — Detailed cost breakdown
5. **Read Tips** — Local insights & useful phrases

Simple 5-step flow from idea to complete plan.
